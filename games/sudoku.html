🎮 How to Play

Click a cell, then press a number. Fill every row, column and 3×3 box with digits 1–9 with no repeats!

⌨️ Controls

  • 1–9 Fill cell
  • Del / 0 Erase
  • Arrow keys Move
  • N Toggle notes

💡 Tips

Start with rows, columns or boxes that already have the most numbers filled in!
